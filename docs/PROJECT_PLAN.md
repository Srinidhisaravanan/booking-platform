## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

Technical Risks:

- Payment Gateway Integration Issues (Medium risk, High impact)
  Mitigation: Early POC, multiple gateway options

- Database Performance at Scale (Medium risk, High impact)
  Mitigation: Performance testing, caching strategy

- Third-party API Dependencies (High risk, Medium impact)
  Mitigation: Circuit breakers, fallback mechanisms

- Security Vulnerabilities (Medium risk, Very High impact)
  Mitigation: Regular security audits, automated scanning

Business Risks:

- Theatre Partner Adoption (Medium risk, Very High impact)
  Mitigation: Pilot program, incentive packages

- Competition from BookMyShow (High risk, High impact)
  Mitigation: Differentiated pricing, superior UX

- Regulatory Changes (Low risk, Medium impact)
  Mitigation: Legal consultation, compliance monitoring

- Market Demand Fluctuation (Medium risk, Medium impact)
  Mitigation: Diverse revenue streams, flexible pricing
